# Deliverable 3: Distribution Plan (40%)

**Question to answer:** How do people discover the platform and use the Skill Market?

**Budget:** $10,000 for the **first month**. Allocate clearly (e.g. $5K Twitter Ads, $3K KOL, $2K events). Organic channels = $0 but need action plan and timeline.

## Requirements

- At least **3–5** specific acquisition channels
- Per channel: Why this channel? Action plan (what, where, who?) + Timeline (which week?) + Estimated reach + Cost + How to measure (metrics)
- At least **1 organic** and **1 paid** channel
- Total paid cost ≤ $10,000 in month 1
- Bonus: Concrete partnership/collab (partner name, reason, mutual benefit)
